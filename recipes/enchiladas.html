Carb Smart Enchiladas

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 can refried beans
  • tomato juice
  • 1 pack of carb smart tortilla shells
  • Shredded cheese (mexican blend)

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Add taco seasoning and some of the tomato juice and simmer for a few minutes
  3. Combine refried beans with taco meat mixture
  4. Add meat and bean mixture to tortilla sheels and roll
  5. Add to baking dish, pour remaining tomato juice over enchiladas, and top with cheese
  6. Bake covered, at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes
  7. Add more cheese, and bake an additional 5 minutes, uncovered
  8. Top with sour cream, salsa, etc.
